### Bio-Electric Energy Flow

Direct Miles' Venom Blast bio-electric energy to short circuit the Alchemax containment grid.

Find any pair of capacitors whose joint capacity meets the shock target.

Return their indices. If impossible, output `-1`.

#### Input Format
- First line contains two integers: $N$ and $Target$.
- Second line contains $N$ space-separated integers.

#### Output Format
- Two space-separated integers, or `-1`.
