### Super-Collider Power Grid

Calculate the power output at step $N$ of Kingpin's super-collider before it destabilizes the city.

The power grid charges according to the Fibonacci sequence:
- $P(0) = 0$
- $P(1) = 1$
- $P(i) = P(i-1) + P(i-2)$

Calculate $P(N)$.

#### Input Format
- A single integer $N$.

#### Output Format
- A single integer representing the step $N$ power.
